Gajar ka Halwa

Description

Gajar ka Halwa is a traditional Indian dessert made with carrots, milk, sugar, and ghee. It is flavored with cardamom and garnished with almonds and raisins. It is a rich and delicious dessert that is perfect for special occasions and festivals.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom
  • Almonds
  • Raisins

Recipe

  1. Grate the carrots.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan.
  3. Add the grated carrots and saute for 5 minutes.
  4. Add milk and cook until the carrots are soft.
  5. Add sugar and cardamom.
  6. Cook until the mixture thickens.
  7. Add almonds and raisins.
  8. Enjoy!
