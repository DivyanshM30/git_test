Shahi Paneer

Description

Shahi Paneer is a rich and creamy Indian curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) in a tomato-based gravy. It is flavored with aromatic spices, cream, and nuts, making it a royal and indulgent dish that is perfect for special occasions and celebrations.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cashews
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Spices

Recipe

  1. Grind tomatoes, onions, and cashews to make a smooth paste.
  2. Heat butter in a pan.
  3. Add the tomato-onion-cashew paste and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add spices and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add cream and paneer cubes.
  6. Cook until the gravy thickens and the paneer is heated through.
