Pav Bhaji

Description

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food made with a spicy mix of mashed vegetables served with buttered pav (bread rolls). It is a delicious and flavorful dish that is perfect for a quick and easy meal.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Capsicum
  • Butter
  • Pav

Recipe

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  2. Heat butter in a pan.
  3. Add chopped onions and saute until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add chopped capsicum, peas, and carrots.
  6. Add pav bhaji masala, salt, and red chili powder.
  7. Add the mashed potatoes and mix well.
  8. Cook for 5-10 minutes.
  9. Toast the pav with butter.
  10. Serve the pav bhaji with buttered pav and chopped onions.
